# MERCADOAGRO.MX Brand Guidelines  

## Color Palette  
- **Primary Colors:**  
  - Green: #4CAF50  
  - Yellow: #FFEB3B  
  - Gold: #FFD700  

## Logo Usage  
- The MERCADOAGRO logo must always maintain clear space around it, equal to the height of the letter "M" in the logo.  
- Do not distort or alter the logo's proportions.  
- Use the logo on a white or light background for best visibility.  

## Typography  
- **Primary Font:**  
  - Font Family: Open Sans  
  - Regular Weight: 400  
  - Bold Weight: 700  
- **Headings:**  
  - Use Open Sans Bold for headings.  
- **Body Text:**  
  - Use Open Sans Regular for body text.  

## Spacing  
- Use consistent spacing of 1.5em for paragraph text and 2em for headings to ensure readability.  
- Maintain margins of at least 20px around elements in layouts.  

## Visual Specifications  
- Imagery should reflect the brand's agricultural theme. Use high-quality images that convey sustainability and growth.  
- Icons should be simple, line-based, and limited to a two-color scheme utilizing the primary colors.
